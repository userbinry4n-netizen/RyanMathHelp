💡 Ryan's Math Tips of the Day!!
📚 TIP #1 - The Quadratic Formula:
Stuck on a quadratic equation? Just remember this formula and you can ALWAYS find the answer!!
x = (-b ± √(b² - 4ac)) / 2a
For ax² + bx + c = 0. Memorize it!! You WILL see it on the test!!
📚 TIP #2 - SOHCAHTOA:
Remembering trig ratios is easy with this trick!!
SOH = Sine is Opposite over Hypotenuse
CAH = Cosine is Adjacent over Hypotenuse
TOA = Tangent is Opposite over Adjacent
📚 TIP #3 - Order of Operations:
Always remember PEMDAS!!
Please Excuse My Dear Aunt Sally
Parentheses → Exponents → Multiply → Divide → Add → Subtract
